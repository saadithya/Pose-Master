Privacy Policy

Pose Master — Last updated: March 2025

Introduction

Pose Master ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Device Identifier: We generate a unique device ID to identify your account and track your credit balance. This ID is randomly generated and is not tied to any personal information.

Photos: Photos you select for pose generation are sent to our secure servers for processing. These images are not stored on our servers after the generation is complete.

Purchase Information: When you purchase credits, we record the product ID and purchase token for verification purposes. We do not collect or store payment card details — all payments are processed through Google Play.

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your credit balance and account
  • To process pose generation requests
  • To verify and fulfill in-app purchases
  • To maintain app functionality and performance

Data Storage & Security

Your device ID and credit balance are stored securely on our servers. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

Photos uploaded for generation are processed in memory and are not persisted to disk on our servers. Generated images are returned directly to your device.

Third-Party Services

Google Play Billing: We use Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. Google's own privacy policy applies to payment processing. We do not have access to your payment method details.

OpenAI API: We use OpenAI's image generation API to create pose variations. Uploaded images are sent to OpenAI for processing under their data usage policies. OpenAI does not use customer API data to train their models.

Data Retention

  • Device ID & Credits: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Uploaded Photos: Not stored after generation completes
  • Purchase Records: Retained for transaction verification
  • Generation Logs: Anonymized metadata retained for analytics

Children's Privacy
